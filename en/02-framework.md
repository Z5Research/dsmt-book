# 🏛️ DSMT: Core Framework

> **Core Proposition**: Researching how humans and AI agents achieve "symbiosis rather than substitution" in organizations.

## Three Core Pillars

### 🧩 Pillar 1: Contextual Governance
**Problem**: Why does AI output always deviate?
**Insight**: The essence is **Context Alignment**. AI lacks organizational memory, leading to "Context Pollution". Managers must become "Context Architects".
**Model**: Three layers of Context Injection (Strategic, Business, Tactical).

### 🛡️ Pillar 2: Computational Trust
**Problem**: How to trust an AI without goodwill?
**Insight**: AI has no goodwill, only objective optimization. Trust must shift from a psychological state to an **Engineering Property**.
**Mechanism**: Input Filtering (Immunity), Execution Isolation (Firewall), Output Arbitration (Adversarial Validation).

### 🔄 Pillar 3: Asynchronous Symbiosis
**Problem**: How to manage a 24/7 AI team?
**Insight**: Managers shift from "Real-time Supervision" to **"Subconscious Management"**. Async collaboration via event-driven orchestration and ball ownership.
