## 🌟 What is DSMT?
**Digital Symbiosis Management Theory (DSMT)** is a new management framework designed for the era of AI and Digital Employees. 
It addresses the fundamental shift from "Human Management" to "Human-AI Symbiosis".
When AI Agents become "Digital Employees," traditional management assumptions (motivation, hierarchy, trust) break down. DSMT offers a new set of laws, tools, and philosophies to build high-performance AI-native organizations.

## 🔥 Core Pillars
- **🌐 Contextual Governance**: Context is power. Alignment over instruction.
- **🛡️ Computational Trust**: Verification is trust. Zero-trust architecture for AI.
- **🔄 Asynchronous Symbiosis**: Asynchrony is freedom. Event-driven orchestration.

## 🚀 Key Concepts
- **Hourglass Organization**: Diagnosing the bottleneck of AI adoption.
- **Inverted Pyramid**: Managers at the bottom, AI at the top.
- **Code is Culture**: Corporate culture written in System Prompts.
- **Management Entropy Reduction**: AI eliminates execution chaos but amplifies decision risks.
